Our Values

___________________________________________________

Our values represent what we stand for as a corporate citizen, a business partner and an employer. They
inspire us and will be embodied by our employees at every level, function of our business. Our prevailing
company values are

Commitment

I bring a 100% effort into everything that I undertake.

I bring a 100% effort into everything that I undertake. I have the desire to achieve my personal and company goals.

Adaptability

I am willing and able to improve myself on a constant basis.

I am willing and able to improve myself on a constant basis. I respond to challenges by being open-minded.

Respect

Respecting the individual is fundamental to all it.

Respecting the individual is fundamental to all my interactions. I always focus on the problem and not on the person.

Enjoyment

I put my mind as well as my heart into work.

I put my mind as well as my heart into work. I build enjoyment into daily activities. I like to work in an energetic and rewarding environment.

-------- OUR VISION --------


The idea of seeing SJCPL as one of the most sought after contractor is what excites us the most. SJCPL shall be a professionally run organisation. The leadership will comprise of substantial individuals able to drive further growth in alignment to the company’s values and culture. We will have created a respectable standing in the market and will be a much sought after contractor for private as well as public sector projects. The quality of our services and the technology used will be identified as the standard benchmark for the construction industry. Employee welfare and happiness will still be a key priority. We will be the most preferred employer in the construction sector. Our employee experience will be such that all employees will feel a sense of familial attachment to the company.
